Our Meals Will Blow Your Mind

We have the finest Michelin-star chefs in the world to create the most elegant and flavorful meals your taste buds will ever experience. We are so confident in the quality of our food that if it doesn't exceed your expectations of sustenance, we'll refund your money - guaranteed.

Peking Duck (北京烤鸭) - Crispy roasted duck served with thin pancakes, hoisin sauce, and scallions.

Mapo Tofu (麻婆豆腐) - Spicy Sichuan dish with soft tofu, minced meat, and a numbing chili sauce.

Xiaolongbao (小笼包) - Soup dumplings filled with pork and broth, famous in Shanghai cuisine.
Butter Chicken - Creamy tomato-based curry with tender chicken, best paired with naan or rice.

Biryani - Aromatic rice dish made with spices, saffron, and marinated meat (chicken, lamb, or fish).

Masala Dosa - Crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with spiced potato filling, served with chutney and sambar.
Sushi (寿司) - Vinegared rice with fresh seafood or vegetables, often served with wasabi and soy sauce.

Ramen (ラーメン) - Noodle soup with different broths (tonkotsu, miso, shoyu) and toppings like pork, egg, and seaweed.

Okonomiyaki (お好み焼き) - Savory Japanese pancake made with cabbage, batter, and various toppings like seafood or bacon.
